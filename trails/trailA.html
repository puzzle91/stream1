Guided Tour A - The Lower Mountains

Experience the beauty of the lakes and rivers Rinjani has to offer and camp under a star-filled sky. Hike A is the shortest and least streneous of hikes. This hike is predominantly for beginners and for older participants. It usually takes around 4-5 days to complete.


Prep: bring proper shoes and a headlamp. If you don’t have a jacket, the porters can provide one if told in advance. Hiking or running shoes and a jacket are absolutely essential and a bathing suit, hiking stick (you can grab one at the start of the trail), and mittens are recommended
